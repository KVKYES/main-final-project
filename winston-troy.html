    WINSTON TROY

    Winston Troy was my solo project, after Heavy Creatures disbanded. The name is my great-geat aunt's name. I just liked how it sounded. I played under this name for a year and a half between 2009-2010. I recorded one EP and went on a small self-booked 3-week solo tour arond the east and midwest. The kindness of many, many awesome friends made this possible! Very (very) shortly after this tour, I became pregnant with my firstborn son. Then, mamarama took over!
